Events

There are 23 events in total, ranging from engineering challenges like Air Trajectory and Electric Vehicle, to science-based competitions such as Anatomy & Physiology, Ecology, and Dynamic Planet: Glaciers. Each participant will take a preliminary test for each event to determine the best match and fit based on their strengths and interests. With such a variety of events, each person can typically expect to compete in 2-3 events alongside 1-2 partners, allowing them to showcase their skills and engage with different areas of science and engineering. Air Trajectory

Build a device to hit a target. Test your engineering skills and precision.

Anatomy & Physiology

Test over integumentary, skeletal, and muscular systems.

Astronomy

Demonstrate understanding of Stellar Evolution and Star Formation.

Bungee Drop

Create Elastic Cord to drop objects as close to the ground without touching it.

Chem Lab

Conduct a Lab and take a test over equilibrium and chemical reactions/stoichiometry.

Codebusters

Crack various ciphers; not directly related to computer science.

Disease Detectives

Investigate various case studies in a test format.

Dynamic Planet: Glaciers

Understand processes in the Earth, with an emphasis on glaciers.

Ecology

Answer questions about specific biomes: Deserts & Grasslands and general ecology.

Electric Vehicle

Build a vehicle powered by electrical energy that aims to quickly follow a track.

Entomology

Identify fossils off of an entomology list and answer questions over them.

Experimental Design

Conduct an experiment and note the entire process.

Forensics

Use tests and given data to incriminate a suspect in a lab form.

Fossils

Identify fossils off of a fossil list and answer questions based on knowledge.

Geologic Mapping

Understand and analyze various maps of Earth and other planets.

Helicopter

Use rubber-powered helicopters to maximize airtime.

Materials Science

Construct a durable puck and answer questions about materials science.

Microbe Mission

Answer questions and analyze data pertaining to microbes.

Optics

Answer questions over optics and angle light to hit a specific target.

Robot Tour

Design a robotic vehicle to navigate a track accurately and reach a target.

Tower

Build a tower with specific requirements; must hold as much weight as it can while being as light as possible.

Wind Power

Build a blade design that generates as much power as possible; Answer questions about physics and natural resources.

Write it Do it

One participant desribes a bizzarre object with words; another participant attempts to reconstruct it.
